    Overview

    The number of web sites has grown dramatically and most of the time they are ready and available to serve the intended purpose but just occasionally they aren’t. Typically the first you know about a problem is when somebody calls to tell you. But how many more people experienced the problem and didn’t say anything.

    Foglight OnDeamnd monitors the basic availability of your web sites giving you the peace of knowing that they are available and being alerted quickly to any problem.

    • Availability Alerts

      Get online alerts, and email notifications if your web site becomes unavailable or very slow to respond.

    • Geographic Testing

      Foglight tests your URLs form multiple locations around the world to enable you to see how users in different locations are likely to experience your web site.

    • Understand Response

      Use the graphical presentation to understand the breakdown between Page Fetch Time, Server Connection Time and DNS Lookup.

    • Manage Multiple URLs

      Test multiple URLs and group them together to gain further insight.

    • Share your Insight with Others

      Use the simple reporting to print and share what you see and find with others and get our daily uptime report emailed to you.
